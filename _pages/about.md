I work on **embodied intelligence** — robot perception, manipulation, and the bridge between foundation models and physical interaction.

My current focus spans **vision-language-action models**, **interactive perception**, and **world models** for robotic agents. I am interested in how robots can build grounded understanding of the world through their own sensorimotor experience, and how large-scale prior knowledge from foundation models can be combined with embodied learning.

Please reach out if you would like to discuss research, collaborations, or open opportunities.
